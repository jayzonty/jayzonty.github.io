Iggy Speaks

Iggy Speaks Privacy Policy

Iggy Speaks is an application that narrates the life of St. Ignatius through the use of QR codes that are placed on the statues of St. Ignatius inside the Ateneo de Manila campus. In order to scan the QR codes, the app will make use of your phone's camera. Upon detection of the QR code, the app makes a simulated call, which prompts the user to answer or decline the call. Upon answering the simulated call, a narration of a part of St. Ignatius' life is then played.

Iggy Speaks does NOT in any way collect personal data from its users. The camera of the mobile phone is used solely for the purpose of scanning the QR codes that are attached to the statues. Upon detection of a QR code, the app only makes a simulated call, and does not in any way access the user's phone number, nor collect any other personal information.
